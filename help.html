Help & Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are needed for a ride?

Valid driving licence, government ID proof and a refundable security deposit.

Is fuel included in the price?

Fuel is usually not included. Return the vehicle with the same fuel level as at pickup.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes. Use the My Rides page and click Cancel Ride for the booking.

What if I face a breakdown or emergency?

Contact our 24×7 roadside support at +91-98765-12345 for immediate assistance.
